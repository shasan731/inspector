Privacy Policy

Effective 16 July 2026.

Used Phone Inspection Assistant works without an account or backend. Inspection data, manual identifiers, seller/customer fields, photos, retained audio, settings, and reports remain in private local storage or a location the user explicitly chooses.

The app has no analytics, advertising, tracking, remote configuration, or uploading crash SDK. It never automatically retrieves IMEI, IMSI, ICCID, phone number, Wi-Fi MAC address, contacts, messages, or call history. A manually entered IMEI is not verified against any external database.

Permissions are requested only on relevant test screens. Exports, sharing, printing, and backups happen only after a user action. Users can delete an inspection, temporary files, or all inspection data in Settings.

Google Play processes payment and purchase information for the non-consumable lifetime Pro upgrade. Google Play's privacy terms apply to that processing. The app locally caches entitlement status and does not log purchase tokens.

No report is manufacturer certification or a guarantee of ownership, IMEI validity, repair history, hidden damage, future reliability, or future performance.
